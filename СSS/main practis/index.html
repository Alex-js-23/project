CHARPTER 1.Down the Rabbit-Hole

H2O M3

I ask the indulgence of the children who may read this book for dedicating it to a grown-up. have a serious reason : he is the best friend I have in the world.. I have another reason: this grown-up understands everything, even books about children. I have a third reason: he lives in where he is hungry and cold . He needs cheering up. If all these reasons are not enough, I will dedicate the book to the child from whom this grown-up grew. All grown-ups were once children - although few of them remember it. And so I correct my dedication:

Once when I was six years old I saw a magnificent picture in a book, called True Stories from Nature, about the primeval forest. It was a picture of a boa constrictor in the act of swallowing an animal. Here is a copy of the drawing.
